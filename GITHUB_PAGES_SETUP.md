# GitHub Pages Setup for Privacy Policy

Your privacy policy has been pushed to GitHub! 

## Enable GitHub Pages (One-time setup)

1. Go to: https://github.com/gvvvinay/costco-bill-splitter/settings/pages

2. Under "Source", select:
   - **Branch:** main
   - **Folder:** / (root)

3. Click **Save**

4. Wait 1-2 minutes for deployment

## Your Privacy Policy URL

Once enabled, your privacy policy will be available at:

**https://gvvvinay.github.io/costco-bill-splitter/privacy-policy.html**

## For Google Play Store

Use this URL in your Play Console:
- Store listing → Privacy Policy → Add URL
- Paste: `https://gvvvinay.github.io/costco-bill-splitter/privacy-policy.html`

## Verification

After enabling Pages, visit the URL above to verify it's working.

---

**Note:** GitHub Pages is free and works automatically with public repositories. If your repo is private, you'll need GitHub Pro or make it public for Pages to work.
